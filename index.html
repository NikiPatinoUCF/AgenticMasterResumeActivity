Niki Patino is a PhD candidate, an English professor, a former zookeeper, a studio owner, a writing coach, and a copy editor. She has written a memoir, trained primates, ghostwritten sixty-thousand-word manuscripts, and opened an aerial arts studio during a pandemic. The same person did all of these things.

Each page below is addressed to a different reader. Nothing has been invented for any of them. The tension between them is left intact.

  • Editorial & Publishing For publishers, journals, copy editing clients, and language analysis roles.
  • Writing Mentor & Coach For individuals seeking 1:1 writing coaching, admissions help, or creative development.
  • Digital Humanities For academic DH programs, computational research contexts, and PhD committees.
  • Entrepreneur & Independent Practice For business partners, studio clients, brand collaborators, and independent operations.
  • Zookeeper & Wildlife For zoos, conservation organizations, and environmental science employers.
